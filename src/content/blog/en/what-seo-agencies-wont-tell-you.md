A few things SEO agencies usually leave unsaid, worth hearing plainly before you sign a contract. No hate for the industry, just honesty.

## "Results in a few months" is not an excuse, it is the truth

SEO works slowly. An honest agency will say so. The catch is that you pay every month from day one, while the result shows much later. That is not a scam, but you need to know it going in.

## Nobody guarantees the top spot

Not in Google, not in ChatGPT. No agency controls the algorithm. Anyone guaranteeing a position either does not understand how it works, or is counting on you not checking.

## Metrics that look great and mean nothing

"Impressions up 300%" sounds brilliant and can bring zero customers. Ask one thing: how many customers, how many real enquiries. The rest is often vanity numbers.

## The world just changed

More and more decisions happen inside an AI answer, not on a Google list. Some agencies still sell old SEO only. Ask plainly what they do about visibility in ChatGPT and Perplexity. If they have no answer, you know where you stand.

## What to do about it

I am not saying "do not work with an agency". I am saying: go in with your eyes open. Ideally with your own independent diagnosis in hand, so you know what actually needs fixing and do not pay for fog.
