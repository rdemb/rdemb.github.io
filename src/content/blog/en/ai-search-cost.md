"What does it cost?" is the first question I get about AI visibility. The honest answer is: it depends. But here are concrete ranges, and what you really pay for.

## Three levels of cost

- **Do it yourself, free.** Checking whether AI knows you, and fixing the basics on your site, costs nothing but your time. I described it in [a separate post](/en/blog/does-chatgpt-recommend-you/).
- **A one-time diagnosis.** Someone X-rays your company and tells you what to fix first. With me, [the X-ray](/preswietlenie/) is €390.
- **Ongoing work.** Content and presence beyond your site build over months. That is either your time or a subscription: from €690/mo with me, usually €1.5k to €10k a month at SEO agencies.

## What you really pay for

Not "AI magic". You pay for time and judgment. Someone regularly creates content that answers your customer's questions, makes sure a machine can read it, and earns mentions in credible sources. The same things that matter in Google.

## Red flags in a quote

- "We guarantee the top spot in ChatGPT." Nobody can guarantee that.
- A high subscription with no clear description of what you get each month.
- A price with no prior diagnosis. How do you quote a repair without knowing what is broken?

## Honestly

The cheapest move is to start with the free test and a one-time diagnosis. Only once you know what actually hurts does it make sense to pay for ongoing work. Not the other way around. [The X-ray](/preswietlenie/) is exactly for this: truth with evidence first, then the decision of how much, or whether, to spend at all.
