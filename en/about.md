I build small computational experiments and describe them without pretending to be an expert.

I learn by building: take an idea, narrow it into a test, add a baseline, and write down the result. I also care about why something fails.

Markets are part of my daily work. Outside that, I write code, test models, and check small technical hypotheses.

This site is a public notebook: projects, positive and negative results, reflections, and limits.

It is not investment advice.
